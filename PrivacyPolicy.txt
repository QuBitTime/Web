QuBit Time Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal data is collected, used and disclosed when you use a QuBit Time app or watch face.

COLLECTION OF PERSONAL DATA
QuBit Time does not collect, use or share any personal data.
However, if you have installed a QuBit Time app or watch face from the Google Play Store, the Google Play Store collects some personal data regarding its services, but not QuBit Time. In this regard, consult the Google Play Services Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy?hl=it
When we talk about “Personal Data” in this Privacy Policy, we are referring to both Device Information and Order Information.


HOW DO WE USE YOUR PERSONAL DATA?
QuBit Time does not collect, use or share any personal data.


YOUR RIGHTS
QuBit Time does not collect, use or share any personal data.

If you reside in Europe, you have the right to access personal data about you on Google and its services and to request that it be updated, corrected or removed. If you wish to exercise this right, please contact Google via its contact details.


DATA RETENTION
QuBit Time does not collect, use or share any personal data.
However, when you place orders through the Google Play Store, the Google Play Store retains Order Information in its records unless and until you request Google to delete such information.

MINORS
Each QuBit Time app or watch face has no target limit for minors and can be used at any age.

CHANGES
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes to our practices, or for other operational, legal or regulatory reasons.
